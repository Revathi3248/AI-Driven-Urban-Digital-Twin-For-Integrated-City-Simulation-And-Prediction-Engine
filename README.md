# AI-Driven-Urban-Digital-Twin-For-Integrated-City-Simulation-And-Prediction-Engine
Integrated City Simulation and prediction engine using AI for smart urban planning and decision-making.
Project Overview

The AI-Driven Urban Digital Twin for Integrated City Simulation and Prediction Engine is an intelligent smart city management system that creates a virtual representation of urban environments. The system integrates real-time data, artificial intelligence, simulation models, and predictive analytics to support efficient urban planning and decision-making.

The platform enables:

Real-time monitoring of urban infrastructure
Traffic congestion prediction and analysis
Multi-agent city simulation
Scenario-based urban planning
AI-powered decision support
Resource optimization and sustainability management

The primary objective of this project is to provide city administrators and planners with a unified platform for monitoring, predicting, and optimizing urban operations.

Problem Statement

Modern cities generate vast amounts of data from transportation systems, environmental sensors, utility networks, and public services. Existing smart city solutions are often domain-specific and operate independently, making it difficult to analyze interactions between different urban systems.

To address these challenges, an integrated Urban Digital Twin framework is required that combines real-time data, simulation, artificial intelligence, and predictive analytics to improve city-scale decision-making.

Objectives
Develop a city-scale digital twin for urban monitoring.
Integrate real-time and historical urban data.
Predict traffic congestion and urban conditions using AI models.
Simulate urban scenarios using multi-agent systems.
Optimize urban operations using reinforcement learning.
Support data-driven decision-making for city administrators.
Key Features
Real-Time Data Integration
Collects data from IoT devices and urban information sources.
Processes and updates city information continuously.
Digital Twin Visualization
Provides a virtual representation of city infrastructure.
Displays real-time urban conditions and system status.
Predictive Analytics
Forecasts traffic congestion and resource demand.
Uses Spatiotemporal Graph Neural Networks (STGNN).
Multi-Agent Simulation
Simulates interactions between vehicles, pedestrians, and infrastructure.
Evaluates urban scenarios before implementation.
Reinforcement Learning Optimization
Optimizes traffic signals and resource allocation.
Improves efficiency through adaptive decision-making.
Decision Support Dashboard
Interactive visualizations and analytics.
Provides insights for planners and administrators.
